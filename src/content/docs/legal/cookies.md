## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function correctly and to provide operational information to site owners.

---

## How We Use Cookies

This is a static documentation site. We do not set first-party cookies, operate behavioral tracking, serve targeted advertising, or use web beacons or tracking pixels.

**Cloudflare Pages**, which hosts this site, may set cookies as part of their infrastructure and security services — including bot detection and DDoS mitigation. These cookies are strictly necessary for site operation and are governed by the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

---

## How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. For instructions, visit your browser's help documentation. Note that restricting Cloudflare's infrastructure cookies may affect site availability or performance.
