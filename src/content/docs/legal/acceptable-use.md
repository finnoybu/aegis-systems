## Automated Access

Automated access to this site is permitted only within the bounds of the site's `robots.txt` file. The following are prohibited without prior written permission:

- Scraping, crawling, or systematically downloading site content beyond what `robots.txt` allows
- Using bots, spiders, or automated tools to index, mirror, or reproduce site content
- Circumventing rate limits, access controls, or technical restrictions
