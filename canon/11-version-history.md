# Version History

Aegis is versioned.

Constitutional change requires explicit declaration.

---

## Versioning Principles

- No silent edits.
- All updates require increment.
- Rationale must be recorded.
- Structural diff must be documented.

---

## Amendment Rule

Constitutional amendment requires:

1. Proposed change document.
2. Rationale.
3. Risk impact analysis.
4. Version increment.
5. Publication record.
